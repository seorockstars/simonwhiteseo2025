Signs Your Website Is on Fire

Recognizing ranking decline early is crucial for effective recovery. Here are the red flags that signal you need emergency intervention:

Sudden Ranking Drop

When your rankings disappeared overnight, it's rarely a coincidence. Sudden drops of 10+ positions across multiple keywords indicate serious underlying issues. This could be due to technical errors, content quality problems, or Google penalty actions.

Manual Penalties & Algorithm Updates

Google penalty recovery requires immediate attention. Manual actions appear in Google Search Console with specific violations. Algorithm update impact can be more subtle but equally devastating, often targeting content quality, user experience, or link profiles.

Traffic Loss After Site Changes

Migration SEO issues and redesign traffic loss are common emergencies. When organic traffic drops after website changes, it usually indicates problems with URL structures, internal linking, or content accessibility.

What's the difference between a penalty and an algorithm hit?

Google penalties are manual actions that appear in Search Console with specific violation notices. Algorithm hits are automated ranking adjustments from core updates that don't generate penalty notifications but still cause significant traffic losses.
